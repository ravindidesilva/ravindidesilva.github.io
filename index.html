TEACHING PHILOSOPHY

“ The secret in education lies in respecting the student” – Ralph Waldo-

As I see learning is not just a single act but it is a process where we should step into many levels. For the success of this process the teacher has a great role to play. I believe that this role of a teacher should not be limited just to a speaker. A teacher should be and interactor, communicator as well as an active participant in the session. It is a skill of the teacher to make the lessons more interactive and effective. According to Ever Garrison a teacher is a compass that activates the magnets of curiosity, knowledge and wisdom in the pupils. As a trainee teacher I have experienced being a versatile teacher would help to make the lesson more effective to the students. This includes having conversations with students, playing fun games, give them chances to ask questions, peer discussions, group activities and many other class room activities.

My primary goal as a teacher, is to make my students stepping ahead each day than the previous day. I firmly believe that for this purpose the dedication and the skills of a teacher are the required key factors. This was experienced by me since I was a child where my school teachers put their fullest effort to shaped me into the person I am today. Being an undergraduate in this course (TESL) filled me with immense experiences and trained me to be a good teacher. What I have learnt from all my teachers and lecturers has taught me to be a versatile teacher who has a duty and responsibility towards his/her students.

One of my major responsibilities as a teacher is to keep my attention on every individual student. The students’ needs may differ from each other though they would sit in a one classroom. Thus understanding each student well is the responsibility of me in my teaching life. Our teaching practicum conducted through an online platform and thus a new form of teaching and learning came into effect. Teaching via online was found very interesting and time saving and it could be considered as a best option in a pandemic situation. The knowledge about the technical aspects of online teaching had to be gained prior to the lessons and it was a fresh experience to the teacher and the learner as well. One of my major responsibilities as a teacher is to keep my attention on every individual student. The students’ needs may differ from each other though they would sit in a one classroom. Thus understanding each student well is the responsibility of me in my teaching life. It is a great pleasure to see the improvement of the students’ and their enthusiasm in the classroom. And it is my duty to make them enjoy the lessons by creating my lessons in the way that they would actively participate. I firmly believe that being with students is a therapy to a teacher.
